I have started to use watir-webdriver and Sikuli (http://sikuli.org/) together in a project. The web application is extremely complex with lots of drag and drop and WYSIWYG editors. In addition, it is not built with test automation in mind, so I had a lot of problems. Until I get the developers to add more support for test automation, I have found out that I can automate the tricky parts with Sikuli and the rest with watir-webdriver. Sikuli is good for doing complex stuff and watir-webdriver sometimes has problems with that. On the other hand, Sikuli proved not to be really good for verification of what happened. At the end, I ended up using watir-webdriver for most of the test automation, calling simple Sikuli skripts from Ruby when needed and checking what happened with watir-webdriver.
