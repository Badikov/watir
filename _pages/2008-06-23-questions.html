Alister Scott, Bret Pettichord, Alan Baird, Charley Baker, Jari Bakken and Rich Downie contributed to questions, mostly at #watir IRC channel. If you have a question or six, leave a comment.

Introduction

  • Something about you.
  • Where do you live?
  • How did you get into:
    • computers (your first computer),
    • software testing,
    • automation/Watir/Ruby.
  • Have you used other programming languages?
  • If so how do you think it compares with Ruby?
  • Why do you like testing?
  • What is that you like the most about testing?
  • A little about the application you are testing. (Jari Bakken)
  • How big are development and test teams at your company?
  • Whick editor/IDE are you using for writing Watir/Ruby code?
  • Which OS are you using?

Watir

  • When did you start using Watir? (Bret Pettichord)
  • What have been using Watir for? (Bret Pettichord) Regression, smoke, unit tests... (Alan Baird)
  • What do you like about Watir? (Bret Pettichord)
  • What don't you like about Watir? (Bret Pettichord)
  • What frameworks have you been using with Watir? Test::Unit, RSpec, WET... (Alan Baird)
  • What would you like to see in future versions of Watir?
  • Have you used other flavours of Watir? (Firewatir, Safariwatir, Watij, Watin...)
  • Have you used other testing tools, open or closed source (Selenium...)? (Rich Downie)
  • If so how do you think it compares with Watir? (Rich Downie)
  • Do other people use your Watir code? (Bret Pettichord)
  • Have you built any tools of your own? (Bret Pettichord)
  • How do you help out with Watir? (Bret Pettichord)
  • Any metric you find relevant to share (time to run tests, number of tests or assertions, lines of code, functionality covered...)?

For Watir flavours

  • How would you compare your flavour of Watir with the Ruby one?
  • What is the current status of your project? (Charlie Baker)
  • What is the roadmap? (Charlie Baker)
  • How big is community for your tool?
  • Do you have documentation?
  • How many developers are working on the tool?
  • Do you know how many users/downloads/installations you have?
  • Would you (and how) convince me to switch to your tool?

Closing

  • Would you share with us interesting/funny testing/bug hunt story?
  • What do you like to do beside testing?
  • Do you listen to podcasts?
  • Any closing thoughts? Something you would like to say to listeners?
  • Do you have a web site/twitter account?
