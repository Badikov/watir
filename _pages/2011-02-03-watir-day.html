Intro

The Watir and Selenium teams are working together to use common technology for the next generations of both projects. Many key Watir contributors will be participating in the Selenium Conference in April in San Francisco.

Since so many people on the Watir team will be San Francisco, we've set aside one day to focus on the Watir project. We are planning a day of talks and collaboration from Watir & Selenium developers and users at the same venue as the Conference on Sunday the 3rd of April, the day before Selenium Conference 2011 begins.
